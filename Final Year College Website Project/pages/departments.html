Computer Sc. & Engineering

As we know the today’s world is dumb without “information”. Almost everything depends on “Historical” as well as “Real time” information. Information includes all forms of processed data – and the processing is not conceivable without computers (in its all forms). There is a big role of Internet for the Economic growth and stability of a prosperous country. Hence we can dream of a bright future for the students of CST. Computer Science and Engineering marked an epoch in IT Industry. CST Students have admittedly knowledge and expertise in Computer science. Applications of CAD, CAM and ancillary exercises should be of utmost priority in industrial hegemony and behavioral science. Engineering by all counts has been immensely activated, overcame itches and problems of' blind spots' of contemporary industries. Computers in Engineering boost up the efficiency more than edge. So, move on with IMPS with greater zeal.
In total 3 years curriculum several subjects like Data Structure & Algorithm, Data Base Management System, Object Oriented Programming, Software Engineering, Computer Organization and Architecture, Operating System, Computer Network, Numerical Methods, Automata Theory, Compiler Design is covered. Students are also aware with some advance topic like Distributed Computing, Cloud Computing, Artificial Intelligence, Image Processing, Soft Computing etc. In the department students are getting prepared for focusing in the IT industry.

Electrical Engineering

Electrical engineering deals with the application of electrical energy to make the world doable by generating and transmitting the electrical power from one corner to other using different types of electrical equipments. It is the most important sector in Engineering that deals with study & application of electricity, electronics and electromagnetism. Generally, Electrical Engineering deals with problems associated with large-scale electrical system like generation transmission and motor control.
The Department of Electrical Engineering is continuing from the date of its inception with a mission of imparting teaching and promoting implementation of practical aspects to build a solid foundation as a part of the Engineering Education. It has the motto to brighten the face of this institution to the centre of excellence in this core field. This Department contains well experienced highly qualified young and dynamic faculties with an ability to provide quality teaching. The Department strives to impart up-todate technical knowledge and skills and produce well-qualified engineers, who will make qualitative difference in this field. Inculcate professional responsibilities to play leadership roles along their diverse career paths and develop capability to utilise engineering skills in industry, research centres, national laboratories and entrepreneurship development. Develop a diverse range of theoretical skills and practical experience, presented in the context of real applications and design experience.
The Diploma is a sure gate way to self employment as well as lucrative employment opportunities in the industry. Electrical Engineers closely maintain and monitor the functioning of motors, dynamos, electrical panels, etc. that greatly impact production and the revenues of any production unit. The program imparts hands-on skills to make students industry ready. After completion of this program, students can also take admission directly to the second year of electrical engineering through lateral entry mode.

Mechanical Engineering

Mechanical engineering is one of the largest, broadest, and oldest engineering disciplines. Mechanical engineers use the principles of energy, materials, and mechanics to design and manufacture machines and devices of all types. They create the processes and systems that drive technology and industry.
The key features of the profession are its breadth, flexibility, and individuality. The career paths of mechanical engineers are largely determined by individual choices, a decided advantage in a changing world. Mechanical engineering is a diverse subject that derives its breadth from the need to design and manufacture everything from small individual parts to large systems . The role of a mechanical engineer is to take a product from an idea to the marketplace. In order to accomplish this, a broad range of skills are needed. The mechanical engineer needs to acquire particular skills and knowledge.
This particular Department is continuing from the date of its inception with a mission of imparting teaching and promoting implementation of practical aspects to build a solid foundation as a part of the Engineering Education. It has the motto to brighten the face of this institution to the centre of excellence in this core field. Civil Engineering

Civil Engineering is a professional engineering discipline that involves Design, Construction and Maintenance of the physical and naturally built environment encompassing roads, bridges, canals, tunnels, dams, sewage systems, buildings, ports and airports. Civil Engineering can be classified as Structural, Water Resources, Environmental, Construction, Transportation, Geo-technical, Materials, Coastal and Municipal. This department of this Institute is actively working in the thrust areas like hazardous waste management, air pollution and monitoring, waste water treatment, environmental audit and impact assessment, Surveying, Highway and Transportation systems, Hydraulic and hydrologic analysis, ground water flow and pollutant transport, water conservation practices and simulation and modelling of water resources systems. Civil engineers are employed in all the major construction projects carried out by the state or central government, the railways, private construction companies, military, engineering services, etc.
Since its inception, it is growing up steadily with the valuable guidance of Administration, co-operation of the Faculties and Heads of other departments, Staff of Office and Hostels. The departmental faculties are well experienced consisting younger and older generations. Very senior professors of eminent universities are also available in this department as advisors. Besides, the laboratories are modern, spacious and well equipped. Students in every year attend the short survey camp and educational tour. Last year many of our students attended industrial training in eminent public and private organizations.
