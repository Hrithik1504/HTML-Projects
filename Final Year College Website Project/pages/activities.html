Activities

TRAINING :

We are building our own industries which are also helping students to take SUMMER TRAINING and to serve and initial placement platform.


SOFT SKILL DEVELOPMENT :

The present scenario to get placement is not tough but most of the employers put forward SOFT SKILLS as an essential requirement which means students should have capabilities to speak English well and to write correct English. These qualities are lacking in most of the students but if any student is keen to develop these soft skills, it can be assured that this can be developed by our system of grooming the students without any extra expenses.


SCHOLARSHIPS :

☀ We are selecting few meritorious & financially backward students for free ship & half free ship in their tution fees.
☀ We also provide information and help the students to grab different scholarships provided by WB. Govt./ Central Govt. A good number of private organizations also provide stipend for which we guide the students.
☀ We also assist minority students to achieve those lengitimate scholarships from Minority Commission.
☀ In case a student who is neither enjoying free ship nor half free ship but unfortunately lost his father/ guardian who is the lone earning member of his/her family, is entitled to get free ship jointly by University and Institution.
☀ Over and above we render financial help to many students as per gravity of the case termed as 'Special Cases'.


LIBRARY FACILITIES :

☀ No student needs to purchase any book as our Library along with Book Bank faciliy assist students to lend 6 - 8 books at time.
☀ Extended working hour of library after college hours(upto 10pm) helps students (especially Hostellers) to access reference books in the spacious reading room.


POWER SERVICE :

Our Institute is equipped with uninterrupted Power Service facility which provides power for 24x7 hrs, both in college and hostel.


HOSTELS :

Each hostel room has attached bath facility. Balanced food and purified water is supplied to students.


HOSTEL FOR FIRST YEAR :

For 1st year boys there is a completely separate hostel outside the college campus and for Girls one separate floor is being allotted to avoid any case of ragging.


RAGGING FREE ATMOSPHERE :

For newly admitted students a complete separate enclosure as class rooms in the institute academic block and separation from seniors in their dwelling places (Hostels) has created a 'NO RAGGING atmosphere'. No incident of ragging of student since inception of this Institute has been recorded.
