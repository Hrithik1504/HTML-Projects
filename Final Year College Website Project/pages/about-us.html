About IMPS Polytechnic College

The IMPS Polytechnic College was founded in the year 2015. This institute is located at natural backdrop of North Bengal far from the madding crowd. Still all the facilities of Siliguri and Jalpaiguri are within easy and comfortable reach. The gorgeous North Bengal being very close to IMPS PTC. The cultural traditions of North Bengal cast a magic spell all around. The students and teachers are deeply imbued with its mission and educational concepts.
The setting is not only serene and solemn, but also intimately attached to nature, which is vibrant with the notes of birds, whispering wind, mesmerizing fragrance of flowers and of course, the positive nod of tree-tops. Any casual visitor to the campus may also be accorded with a warm welcome from patrons, pupils and personal. Out and out it is humane.
IMPS Polytechnic College is at Jalpaiguri District situated at Chewara Para, (Near Rani Nagar BSF Camp) Post – Patkate, P.S. Kotwali Dist – Jalpaiguri, an endeavour by the Chairman of IMPS Educational Trust Mr. Bablu Bhattacharjee (M. Tech. M.B.A Charterd Engineer).
The campus is linked with communications spreading over different points of transit Again the campus is just entirely free from all sorts of pollution and disturbances. The spacious classrooms, well-ventilated laboratories well equipped workshops, rich library, tranquil reading room, well furnished tutorial halls, administrative office, internet services, pay canteen, common rooms, hostel facilities, etc. are available to scholars. Besides these there are ample scope for cultural programs, games, sports, debates, counseling cell, mock interviews, recreational breaks, etc.
The college has a full-fledged Placement and Training Centre. This centre also organizes several training programs related to development of soft skills to our college students. The Placement and Training Centre has enabled our college students to get job offers from many leading organizations even before completion of their formal degree.
Our students from neighbouring area and other state will pour in large numbers to define IMPS PTC, Jalpaiguri a mirror of excellence throughout the country. It is an institution laden with lots of commitments and host of convenience and aspiration focussed on delivering quality education in North Bengal and surroundings. We’re a part of large family named IMPS Educational Trust owning institutions of diversified domain such as educational institutes as well as hotel.
